About Us

Welcome to Krishna Automobile – your trusted partner for two-wheeler repairs since 1998. Founded by Manishbhai Patel, our shop has grown from a small local business to one of the most reputed and reliable service centers in Ahmedabad. With over two decades of experience, Krishna Automobile has built a reputation for delivering top-notch, affordable, and timely repairs, making us the go-to destination for riders across the city.

We pride ourselves on staying ahead of the curve when it comes to innovation and technology. That’s why we’re thrilled to offer specialized repair and maintenance services for the latest BS6 and BS7 vehicles, ensuring that your two-wheeler meets the highest standards of environmental efficiency and performance. Whether it’s regular servicing, engine overhauls, or complex electrical and mechanical repairs, our experienced technicians handle it all with precision and care.

Our commitment to quality service extends beyond just repairs. At Krishna Automobile, we believe in creating lasting relationships with our customers by offering personalized advice and solutions tailored to their specific needs. We use only genuine parts and the latest tools, ensuring that every vehicle we service performs at its best for miles to come.

Located conveniently beside the AMC office on Girivr Rajdhani Road, Ramvadi, Isanpur, Ahmedabad, Krishna Automobile is easily accessible for residents across the city. So, whether you’re facing a minor issue or need a full-service checkup, we’re here to help you get back on the road quickly and safely.

Choose Krishna Automobile – because your two-wheeler deserves the best care!
